# Multicast Problem Fallacy

Ali Ghodsi's cautionary analogy from his PhD: the "multicast problem" was the hottest networking challenge of 2000 that no one has heard of today because bandwidth costs collapsed. Lesson: the currently-hyped technical problems (chips, AGI race) may turn out to be irrelevant, while unexpected applications (like Airbnb, 9 years after the internet) will create real value.
