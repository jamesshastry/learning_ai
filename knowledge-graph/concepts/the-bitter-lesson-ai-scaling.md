# The Bitter Lesson (AI Scaling)

Rich Sutton's observation that, historically in AI, simply throwing more compute at problems, combined with general-purpose methods, has consistently yielded better results than sophisticated, human-engineered solutions, leading to a continuous demand for more computational power.
