# Chemical Accuracy

A precision threshold of approximately 1 kilocalorie per mole, representing the accuracy needed for computational chemistry predictions to match experimental results. Achieving this enables computational replacement of laboratory experiments. A very high bar due to the small energy differences involved in chemical reactions relative to total system energy.
