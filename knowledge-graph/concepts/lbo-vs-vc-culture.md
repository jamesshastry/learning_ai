# LBO vs VC Culture

The fundamental cultural tension between venture capital (invest in entrepreneurs, focus on growth, build new things) and leverage buyout (entry price, efficiency, firing people). AI creates LBO-like opportunities but pursuing them splits VC culture and mission.
