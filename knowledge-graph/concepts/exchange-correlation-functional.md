# Exchange Correlation Functional

A universal but analytically unknown function within density functional theory that captures quantum exchange and correlation effects on electron energy. One functional describes all of chemistry. 60+ years of handcrafted approximations exist (~800 functionals). Microsoft's Scala learns it via ML, achieving chemical accuracy with cubic scaling.
