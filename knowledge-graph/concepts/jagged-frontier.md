# Jagged Frontier

Ethan Mollick's concept that AI capability is not uniformly distributed across tasks — AI is excellent at some skills (e.g., coding, certain analysis) but terrible at others (e.g., complex support, nuanced judgment), creating an uneven "jagged" capability frontier rather than a smooth improvement curve.
