# SaaS Apocalypse

The Wall Street narrative that AI will one-shot all SaaS companies, causing a broad sell-off of software stocks. Ben Horowitz argues this conflates companies with deep moats (global supply chains, specialized sales channels) with those whose only defensibility was code and UI.
