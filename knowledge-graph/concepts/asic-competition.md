# ASIC Competition

The competitive landscape of custom AI chips (Google TPU, Meta MTIA, Amazon Trainium, etc.) that could challenge Nvidia's GPU dominance. A breakout ASIC success would be the biggest repricing event in the semis layer and could accelerate the triangle flip.
