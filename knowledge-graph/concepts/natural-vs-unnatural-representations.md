# Natural vs Unnatural Representations

Natural representations (video, audio) originate from physical phenomena with high redundancy and dimensional richness. Unnatural representations (text) are human-engineered for efficient communication with minimal redundancy. AI should learn from natural representations first, as human babies do, to build foundational intelligence.
