# Product-Market Fit S-Curve

The four phases companies traverse: (1) Finding product-market fit through rapid experimentation, (2) Post-PMF process and consistency building, (3) Hypergrowth requiring simultaneous scaling and expansion, (4) Big tech innovator's dilemma requiring zero-to-one innovation against inertia.
