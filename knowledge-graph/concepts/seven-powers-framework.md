# Seven Powers Framework

Hamilton Helmer's framework identifying seven durable competitive advantages (economies of scale, brand, switching costs, network effects, cornered resource, counter-positioning, process power). In the AI era, while barriers to entry and switching costs decrease, moats from data, scale, brand, and trust remain powerful differentiators.
