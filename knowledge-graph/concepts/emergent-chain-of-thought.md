# Emergent Chain-of-Thought

The discovery that model reasoning (thinking step-by-step, self-correcting, exploring multiple approaches) emerges spontaneously from RL training in constrained environments without being explicitly taught. Chain-of-thought was not designed or programmed; it appeared as models were optimized against verifiable rewards.
