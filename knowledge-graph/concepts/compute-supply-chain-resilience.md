# Compute Supply Chain Resilience

The strategic imperative for the AI compute supply chain to avoid single-threading on any one vendor or component. TSMC's wafer allocation policy structurally ensures multiple chip vendors exist. The deepest choke point is ASML, the sole manufacturer of extreme ultraviolet lithography machines.
