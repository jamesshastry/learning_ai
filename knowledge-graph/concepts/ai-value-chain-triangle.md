# AI Value Chain Triangle

The distribution of revenue across the AI ecosystem layers (semis, infrastructure, models, apps), currently shaped as an inverted triangle with semis capturing ~75% of revenue — opposite to the mature cloud ecosystem where apps/SaaS dominate.

## Related Concepts

- [[Supercycle Comparison]] (related to) — Historical supercycles (internet, mobile, cloud) all evolved from infra-heavy to app-heavy revenue distribution.
- [[Marginal Cost of Intelligence]] (enables) — The high marginal cost of AI inference is a key reason the triangle hasn't flipped.
