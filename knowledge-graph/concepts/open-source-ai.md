# Open Source AI

AI models with publicly available weights and architectures that can be inspected, fine-tuned, and deployed independently. Jensen argues open models are essential for AI safety (you cannot secure a black box), democratization of AI across languages and domains, and enabling cybersecurity defense through swarms of cheap, specialized models.
