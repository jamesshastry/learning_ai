# Pre-Standardization Era

The current phase of compute infrastructure analogous to railroads before gauge standardization (1886) or electricity before AC/DC resolution (1907). Characterized by rising prices, hoarding by large players, lack of common standards, and the need for institutions to enforce fungibility.
