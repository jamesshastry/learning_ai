# Modular Data Centers

Self-contained, factory-manufactured AI data center units (500 kW air-cooled or 2 MW liquid-cooled) that can be deployed in fleets at diverse power sites. Reduces construction costs 30-50% through centralized manufacturing, standardization, and lower labor requirements.
