# Token Factory Economics

Ali Ghodsi's prediction that frontier model providers will operate like Amazon.com's book-selling business — high volume, economies of scale, but tiny gross margins and small operating margins. Very few companies will sustain this model, and the business will consolidate to an oligopoly.
