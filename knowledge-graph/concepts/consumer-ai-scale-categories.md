# Consumer AI Scale Categories

Three tiers of consumer app scale: mandatory utilities (3B+ users, e.g., WhatsApp, Chrome), social platforms (1.5-2B users, e.g., Instagram, TikTok), and niche apps (500M-1B users, e.g., Spotify, Twitter). ChatGPT currently sits at niche app scale, heading toward social.
