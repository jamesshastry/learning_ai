# Willingness to Pay Curve

The economic dynamic where AI providers initially sold tokens at a loss (negative gross margins), betting that inference costs would drop while intelligence and willingness to pay would increase. This crossover has now occurred — labs like Anthropic and OpenAI have gone from negative to positive gross margins as capabilities crossed a threshold of usefulness.
