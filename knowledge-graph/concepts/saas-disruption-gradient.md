# SaaS Disruption Gradient

The spectrum of vulnerability for existing software companies: (1) companies with data moats and core-loop positioning (cybersecurity) are most robust; (2) workflow software that hasn't innovated in 10 years is most vulnerable; (3) any company can survive if it innovates, leverages existing data/customers/scale, and adapts its pricing/cost structure.
