# Labor as AI Bottleneck

The acute shortage of electricians, welders, plumbers, and construction workers needed to build AI data centers at scale. Labor is the largest single cost item at $4.7M/MW, costs are rising due to competition between projects, and there are not enough tradespeople to staff multiple gigawatt-scale builds simultaneously.
