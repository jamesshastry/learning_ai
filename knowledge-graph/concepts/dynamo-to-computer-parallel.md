# Dynamo-to-Computer Parallel

Historical pattern from a 1990 Stanford research paper showing that the electric motor took 40 years (1880-1920) to impact factory productivity because organizations simply swapped steam engines for electric ones without redesigning processes. The same pattern repeated with PCs (used as typewriters) and is now repeating with AI.
