# Jailbreak Attacks

Techniques for bypassing LLM safety constraints through creative prompting strategies such as reframing context, requesting translations, claiming information is no longer secret, or using indirect queries. Different models show vastly different vulnerability levels.

## Related Concepts

- [[Agent Safety]] (threatens) — Jailbreaks are more dangerous for agents that can take real-world actions.
- [[Representation Engineering]] (defended by) — Representation engineering offers a defense mechanism against jailbreaks.
- [[Adversarial Attack]] (text equivalent of) — Jailbreaking is the text-domain equivalent of adversarial attacks on vision models.
