# Generative vs Pre-Recorded Computing

The fundamental shift in computing from serving pre-recorded, retrieval-based content (images, videos, compiled software) to generating contextually relevant, real-time content. This shift changes every layer of the computing stack and requires fundamentally different architectures and energy profiles.
