# Computer Hacking and Fraud Act (CFAA)

A U.S. federal law (18 U.S. Code § 1030) that prohibits unauthorized access to protected computers, creating legal complexities and ambiguities regarding what constitutes 'authorization' for security researchers and bug bounty participants.
