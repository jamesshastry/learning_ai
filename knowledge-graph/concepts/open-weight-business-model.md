# Open Weight Business Model

BFL's commercial model of releasing open-weight models at multiple tiers: fully open Apache 2.0 (Schnell), open weights with commercial license (Dev), and API-only (Pro). Works especially well when aesthetic preferences vary widely by audience, enabling customer customization of the last mile.
