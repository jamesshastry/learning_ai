# Compute-Revenue Correlation

The observed pattern at Anthropic where every time new compute infrastructure was brought online, capabilities improved within 60-90 days followed by a predictable revenue jump. Represents a roughly 10x value transformation from hardware asset (3-4x revenue multiple) to software revenue (30-40x multiple).
