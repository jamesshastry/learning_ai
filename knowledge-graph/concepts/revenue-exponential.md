# Revenue Exponential

Anthropic's revenue trajectory of $3.5B (Jan) → $8B (Feb) → $10.5B (Mar) annualized in 2026, driven entirely by product quality crossing a capability threshold rather than salesforce expansion. Described as an "Oppenheimer moment" proving that revenue can scale on the same exponential as intelligence.
