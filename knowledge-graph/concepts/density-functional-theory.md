# Density Functional Theory

An exact reformulation of quantum mechanics (Schrodinger's equation) that replaces the exponentially expensive wave function with the electron density, a function of only 3 spatial dimensions. Reduces computational cost from exponential to cubic in electron count. Nobel Prize (Kohn, 1998). Accounts for ~1/3 of scientific supercomputer workloads.
