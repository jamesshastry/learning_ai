# Vertical Integration Advantage

Historical pattern where the biggest winners of each technology supercycle were vertically integrated players: Google (internet, ~$3T), Apple (mobile, ~$2.5T), Meta (social, ~$2T). This suggests the biggest AI winner may also be vertically integrated across the stack.
