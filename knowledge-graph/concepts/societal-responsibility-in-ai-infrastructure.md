# Societal Responsibility in AI Infrastructure

The obligation of technologists and infrastructure builders to ensure that the deployment of AI infrastructure provides uplift to local communities and the environment, considering factors like noise, water usage, power grid impact, job creation, and the ethical implications of AI's societal transformation.
