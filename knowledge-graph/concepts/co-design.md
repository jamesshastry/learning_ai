# Co-Design

The practice of simultaneously optimizing algorithms, software frameworks, compilers, and hardware architecture together rather than in isolation, yielding exponential performance gains. Originated at Stanford with RISC (Hennessy) and extended by NVIDIA across the entire compute stack.
