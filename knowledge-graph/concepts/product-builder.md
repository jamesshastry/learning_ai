# Product Builder

The emerging role merging traditional PM, design, and engineering skills into a hands-on builder who exercises judgment on what to build and why, replacing the siloed information-moving PM. Companies increasingly hire for this hybrid skill regardless of title.

## Related Concepts

- [[Iteration Speed as Moat]] (enables) — Product builders who can ship directly iterate faster than teams with PM intermediaries.
