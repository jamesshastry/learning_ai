# Pre-Training Data Wall

The challenge that pre-training models have consumed nearly all available internet-scale data. Labs are resorting to buying old libraries to scan books, investing in synthetic data generation, and developing architectural advances to extract more learning from existing data. This data wall is a primary driver of the shift toward post-training scaling.
