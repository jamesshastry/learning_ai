# Supercycle Comparison

Framework comparing AI to prior technology revolutions (internet ~25 years ago, mobile ~20 years ago, cloud ~10 years ago) in terms of value chain evolution, timing, and how long it took for the revenue distribution to shift from infrastructure-heavy to application-heavy.
