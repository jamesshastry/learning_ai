# Data Center Cost Stack

The full cost structure of an AI data center: ~$20M/MW for building and power plant (labor $4.7M, gas plant $2-3M, electrical/mechanical equipment, materials, soft costs) plus ~$40M/MW for IT infrastructure (GPUs $30M, networking $4M, CPUs/storage $3M, fit-out $3M) = ~$60M/MW total, or $60B per gigawatt.
