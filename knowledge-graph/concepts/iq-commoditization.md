# IQ Commoditization

Brad Gerstner's thesis that raw cognitive intelligence (IQ) becomes commoditized as AI systems surpass human-level reasoning, while emotional intelligence (EQ) — networking, persuasion, leadership, team formation — becomes the differentiating and increasingly valuable human capability.
