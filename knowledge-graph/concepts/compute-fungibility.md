# Compute Fungibility

The degree to which compute resources can be substituted for each other. Currently very low — different GPU generations (H100/GB200/B300) and manufacturers (AMD/NVIDIA) are not interchangeable. Unlike electricity (megawatts are megawatts), compute lacks common units, standard interfaces, and buyer substitutability.
