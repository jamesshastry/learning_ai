# Inference Cost Deflation

The ~99% decline in inference cost over 2.5 years driven by three factors: supply chain improvements (TSMC lithography, packaging), engineering innovation (quantization, chip design), and power efficiency. Described as the most deflationary technology ever observed, though model size growth partially offsets gains.
