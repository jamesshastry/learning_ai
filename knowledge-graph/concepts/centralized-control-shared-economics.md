# Centralized Control Shared Economics

a16z's innovation of maintaining a single decision-maker (centralized control) while distributing financial returns across partners (shared economics). This enabled rapid organizational change, scaling into new categories, and avoiding the consensus paralysis of traditional VC partnerships.
