# Bug Bounty Programs

Programs that offer financial rewards to security researchers for discovering and reporting vulnerabilities or 'bugs' in a company's systems, incentivizing external collaboration to enhance security.

## Key Points from Sources

- **cs153 Lecture 01**

## Related Concepts

- [[Responsible Disclosure Policies]] (part of) — Bug bounty programs are an advanced form of responsible disclosure, adding financial incentives.
- [[Computer Hacking and Fraud Act (CFAA)]] (related to) — These programs operate in a legal grey area regarding 'authorized access' under CFAA for external researchers.
