# GPU Spot Price Dynamics

The counterintuitive trend where H100 GPU spot prices have risen above their original launch price (3 years later) due to explosive agent-driven demand, with Blackwell following the same trajectory. This challenges the assumption that older compute quickly loses value and supports longer depreciation periods beyond the standard 6 years.
