# Consumer AI Monetization

The challenge of monetizing consumer AI applications, where ChatGPT monetizes at ~$10/user/year compared to Google's $100/user/year and Meta's $70/user/year. Subscription models may be insufficient; advertising with superior intent understanding could be the key unlock.
