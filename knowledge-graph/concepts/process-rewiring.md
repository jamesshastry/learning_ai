# Process Rewiring

The practice of redesigning organizational processes from first principles to leverage AI, rather than simply overlaying AI on existing workflows. Illustrated by Databricks reducing connector development from 9 months (1 connector) to 1 quarter (7 connectors) not through AI alone but through process changes in requirements gathering, testing, and team structure.
