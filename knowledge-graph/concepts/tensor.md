# Tensor

A generalization of scalars (rank-0), vectors (rank-1), matrices (rank-2) to arbitrary dimensions. Color images are rank-3 tensors (height × width × channels), videos are rank-4 tensors. Any rank-N tensor is a list of rank-(N-1) tensors.
