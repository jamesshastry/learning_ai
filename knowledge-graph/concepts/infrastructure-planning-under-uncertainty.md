# Infrastructure Planning Under Uncertainty

The complex challenge of forecasting and provisioning compute capacity (e.g., gigawatts of power, chips, buildings) years in advance due to long lead times, leading to risks of under- or over-prediction and requiring dynamic, daily replanning based on new information and changing demands.
