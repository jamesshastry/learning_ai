## Detailed Notes

### Overview and Context [00:00–15:00]
Dan Fu presents on efficient architectures and hardware-aware model design, discussing the intersection of systems and ML research. Covers his work on state-space models, the importance of understanding hardware constraints when designing architectures, and research methodology for systems-ML projects. This lecture is part of the broader CS336 curriculum on building language models from scratch, emphasizing both theoretical understanding and practical implementation skills.

### Core Technical Content [15:00–45:00]
The main technical content covers Hardware-Aware Design, Systems-ML Co-Design, Efficient Architectures. Each topic is presented with both theoretical motivation and practical implementation details, following the course philosophy of understanding through building.

### Advanced Topics and Tradeoffs [45:00–01:10:00]
The lecture explores tradeoffs between Hardware-Aware Design and alternative approaches. Real-world examples from open models (Llama, DeepSeek, Qwen) illustrate how these decisions play out at scale.
